# WashSetu Brand Foundation

## Brand Name
`WashSetu`

- Meaning: `Setu` means bridge, so the name reflects a bridge between customers and trusted neighborhood laundry partners.
- Positioning: Smart, reliable, and local-first laundry slot booking.

## Value Proposition
Book laundry slots in seconds, skip queues, and track every order from pickup to delivery.

## Audience
- Busy students and professionals who want predictable laundry timings.
- Families looking for trusted nearby laundry services.
- Laundry vendors who need better demand visibility and slot management.

## Brand Pillars
- Reliability: confirmed slots and transparent status updates.
- Simplicity: frictionless booking flow in under a minute.
- Trust: clear pricing, status history, and accountable vendors.
- Local convenience: discover and book nearby laundry partners.

## Voice And Tone
- Clear and direct.
- Friendly and supportive.
- Operationally confident (no vague promises).

## Visual Direction
- Primary: blue (`#2563EB`) and white for trust and cleanliness.
- Secondary: emerald (`#10B981`) for confirmation/sustainability signals.
- Accent: indigo (`#4F46E5`) for premium highlights.
- Icon style: minimal rounded shapes with a wash drum + bridge motif.

## Messaging Snippets
- App store subtitle: Smart laundry slot booking for busy days.
- Hero line: Your laundry, on your schedule.
- Booking CTA: Reserve slot.
- Tracking CTA: Track order live.
- Vendor CTA: Manage slots and grow bookings.
